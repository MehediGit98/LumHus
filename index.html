About Us

Welcome to LumHus - your Enlightened House for Design & Ideas. We are a multi-disciplinary design and consultancy firm dedicated to delivering exceptional solutions across architecture, interior design, lighting, software development, and management consultancy.

Our team of experienced professionals combines creativity with technical expertise to transform your vision into reality. With a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and client satisfaction, we strive to exceed expectations in every project we undertake.

From conceptual design to final implementation, we work closely with our clients to ensure that every detail aligns with their goals and aspirations.
